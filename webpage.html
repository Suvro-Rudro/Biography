Personal Background

Suvro Sarker Suvo was born in Khulna, Bangladesh. He began his academic journey at Khulna Model School and College, where he successfully completed his JSC in 2011. He then continued to flourish at the prestigious Bangladesh Navy School and College in Khulna, completing both his SSC in 2014 and HSC in 2016 under the Jessore Education Board. These formative years played a pivotal role in nurturing his curiosity, discipline, and love for knowledge.

He comes from a humble and devoted family. His father, Narayan Chandra Sarker, is a respected pharmacist known for his dedication to serving the local community. His mother, Swapna Sarker, is a loving kindergarten teacher who inspires young minds with compassion and care. Both of his parents are deeply religious, leading their lives with integrity, faith, and a profound sense of moral values. Suvro’s strong ethical foundation and empathetic nature are greatly influenced by the graceful spirituality and unwavering principles upheld by his parents.

He has a beloved sister named Puspita Sarker, with whom he shares a deep bond of friendship and support.

Biography

Suvro is currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Sanitation at KUET (Khulna University of Engineering & Technology). He holds a B.Sc. in Geography and Environment from Islamic University, Kushtia, Bangladesh. During his undergraduate years, he demonstrated remarkable leadership and team spirit by winning university-level tournaments in both football and cricket on three occasions. In addition, he is recognized as an agile and skilled badminton player.

His academic and extracurricular achievements together reflect a balanced personality that combines intellectual pursuits with athletic excellence. With an effortlessly charming and warm demeanor, Suvro is often admired for his attractive personality—marked by confidence, empathy, and an inspiring presence that leaves a lasting impression on those around him.

He believes that discipline, passion, and resilience—shaped through both academic challenges and team sports—are foundational to achieving long-term success.
